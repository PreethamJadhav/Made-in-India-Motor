A Century in the Making

Indian mobility didn't start with SUVs and sport bikes — it was decades in the making.

1901

Royal Enfield begins motorcycle production in England — its India legacy would follow decades later.

1945

Tata, Mahindra, and Bajaj all lay their founding roots in the same post-war year.

1955

Royal Enfield begins Indian production in Chennai — a partnership that would outlive the UK parent.

1983

Maruti Udyog rolls out its first car, changing what "affordable" meant for Indian households.
